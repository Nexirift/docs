# Nexicontext

Nexicontext is a powerful feature that enables accounts to supplement posts with essential contextual information that may be missing from the original content. This helps provide clarity, additional facts, or important background details that enhance understanding.

## Rules

<BadgeWrapper selected="Specific to Nexirift" />

When using Nexicontext on the official Nexirift instance, accounts must adhere to these important guidelines:

- The provided context must be directly relevant to the original post and address specific information gaps.
- The provided context must cite highly credible, verifiable sources that support the additional information.
- The provided context must maintain respectful language and avoid offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- The provided context must be genuine and must not contain spam, promote scams, or include misleading information.
